Ed-Quiz

Study tells that kids spending more time with screen media than books.

Ed-Quiz offers the parents to evaluate the performance of the children's studies through Digital Technology in 3 simple steps.

  1. Parents can prepare a set of questions from a textbook grouped by subjects in an MCQ format
  2. Parents can assign and share the Quiz link to their children via WhatsApp, Facebook, or even direct link.
  3. Children can take the Quiz interactively with the Web or Mobile through which their performance can be evaluated.
